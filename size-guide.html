Size Guide

Find your perfect fit with our detailed size guide

How to Measure

Width

Measure the width of the bag from side to side at its widest point.

Height

Measure from the bottom of the bag to the top of the main compartment.

Depth

Measure from front to back at the base of the bag.

Strap Drop

Measure from the top of the strap to the top of the bag.

Sizing Tips

Consider Your Needs

Think about what you'll carry daily and choose a size that accommodates your essentials with some extra space.

Check Device Compatibility

Ensure the bag dimensions can fit your devices like phones, tablets, or laptops if needed.

Measure Twice

Double-check your measurements and compare them with our size charts for the best fit.

Ask for Help

Contact our customer service if you need assistance with choosing the right size.
